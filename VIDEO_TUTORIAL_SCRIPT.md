## 🎥 PART 3: FIRST STEPS (5 minutes)

### Scene 3.1: Learn Stock Basics (1.5 minutes)
**Visual**: Click "Learn: Stock Basics", show tabs

**Script**:
```
"Let's start with the basics. Click 'Learn: Stock Basics'.

[Click and wait for page to load]

You'll see 4 tabs here. Let me show you the most important one - 
Momentum Strategy.

[Click 'Momentum Strategy' tab]

This explains the core concept: Momentum Score equals ROC times 
Trend Slope.

ROC is Rate of Change - how much the price changed over time.

Trend Slope measures the strength and direction of the trend.

When you multiply them together, you get a momentum score. Higher 
score means stronger momentum.

This is what professional traders use. Now you'll learn it too.

Read through all 4 tabs when you have time. For now, let's try it 
with a real stock."
```

## 🎬 PRODUCTION NOTES

### Recording Setup:

**Option 1: Screen Recording Only**
- Use OBS Studio (free) or Camtasia
- Record at 1920x1080 resolution
- 30 fps is fine
- Enable system audio for clicks

**Option 2: Screen + Webcam**
- Picture-in-picture with your face in corner
- Makes it more personal
- Use good lighting
- Look at camera when speaking

**Option 3: Voiceover**
- Record screen first
- Add voiceover later
- Easier to edit
- Can re-record audio if needed

---

### Audio Tips:

1. **Use a good microphone**
   - USB microphone ($30-50)
   - Or smartphone with good mic
   - Quiet room, no echo

2. **Speak clearly and slowly**
   - Your son may watch multiple times
   - Pause between sections
   - Emphasize key points

3. **Be warm and encouraging**
   - This is from father to son
   - Show pride and support
   - Make it personal

---

### Visual Tips:

1. **Cursor movements**
   - Move slowly and deliberately
   - Highlight what you're clicking
   - Pause after clicks to show result

2. **On-screen text**
   - Use large, readable font
   - High contrast (white on dark or vice versa)
   - Keep text on screen 3-5 seconds

3. **Pacing**
   - Don't rush
   - Wait for pages to load
   - Give time to absorb information

---

### Editing Tips:

1. **Cut mistakes**
   - It's okay to re-record sections
   - Edit out long pauses
   - Keep it flowing

2. **Add chapters**
   - YouTube allows chapters
   - Makes it easy to jump to sections
   - Use the 5 parts as chapters

3. **Add captions**
   - Auto-generate on YouTube
   - Review and correct
   - Helps if audio isn't perfect

---

### Publishing Options:

**Option 1: YouTube (Unlisted)**
- Upload as unlisted video
- Only people with link can watch
- Easy to share
- Free hosting

**Option 2: Google Drive**
- Upload video file
- Share link with your son
- Private and secure
- No public access

**Option 3: Loom**
- Quick screen recording
- Automatic hosting
- Easy sharing
- Free for personal use
