# 🎥 Video Tutorial - Quick Reference Card

## 📊 Video Specs
- **Duration**: 18-20 minutes
- **Format**: MP4, 1920x1080, 30fps
- **Sections**: 5 parts
- **Tone**: Warm, educational, fatherly

---

## 🎬 5-Part Structure

| Part | Topic | Duration | Key Points |
|------|-------|----------|------------|
| 1 | Introduction | 2 min | What is this, why it matters |
| 2 | Quick Tour | 3 min | Navigate sidebar, homepage |
| 3 | First Steps | 5 min | Calculate momentum, analyze stocks |
| 4 | Advanced | 5 min | Technical analysis, strategies |
| 5 | Tips & Close | 3 min | Learning tips, getting help |

---

## 🎯 Key Messages (Repeat Throughout)

1. ✅ **Educational** - Learn first, trade later
2. ✅ **Built-in Help** - User Manual has everything
3. ✅ **Take Time** - No rush, learn at your pace
4. ✅ **Safe Practice** - No real money needed
5. ✅ **Dad's Support** - I'm here to help

## 🎨 Visual Elements

### On-Screen Text Style:
- Font: Arial or Helvetica
- Size: 48-72pt
- Color: White with black outline
- Duration: 3-5 seconds each

### Cursor Highlights:
- Use yellow circle around cursor
- Slow, deliberate movements
- Pause on important elements

### Transitions:
- Simple fade between sections
- 0.5 second duration
- No fancy effects needed
