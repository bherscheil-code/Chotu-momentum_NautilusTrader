## 🎥 Video Recording Guide

### Equipment Needed:
- Computer with screen recording software
- Microphone (USB mic or good headset)
- Quiet room
- 1-2 hours of time

### Software Options (Free):
- **OBS Studio** (Windows/Mac/Linux)
- **Loom** (Web-based, easiest)
- **QuickTime** (Mac only)
- **Xbox Game Bar** (Windows 10/11)

### Recording Steps:
1. Open VIDEO_TUTORIAL_SCRIPT.md
2. Deploy app locally or use deployed version
3. Start screen recording
4. Follow script section by section
5. Speak slowly and clearly
6. Show each feature as described
7. Stop recording after closing

### Editing Steps:
1. Watch full recording
2. Cut out mistakes/long pauses
3. Add on-screen text (optional)
4. Add chapter markers
5. Export as MP4 (1080p)

### Publishing Steps:
1. Upload to YouTube (unlisted) or Google Drive
2. Test video plays correctly
3. Copy shareable link
4. Send to your son

**Total Time**: 2-3 hours (recording + editing)
